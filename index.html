Forecasting of wind power generated using various Algorithm

By Aditeya Raj, Shirish Bajpai, Anand Raj and Ashish upadhayay
Under the guidance of Dr S Sumathi (HoD EEE, RNS institute of technology)

The main objective of this project is to offer developing methods of long-term wind speed forecasting, which can provide tendency analysis and reference for wind power equipment operation and wind power system planning. A simple and efficient forecasting method for long-term wind power forecasting is significant, reliable and highly desirable for the development of the wind energy systems.

These are the models used in our project for forecasting.
